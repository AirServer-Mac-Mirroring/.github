## What is AirServer for Mac?

AirServer is a powerful application that turns your Mac into a universal wireless receiver compatible with AirPlay, Google Cast, and Miracast. It allows you to mirror or stream content directly from iPhones, iPads, Android devices, Chromebooks, and Windows laptops to your Mac screen without cables or additional hardware.

The software supports high-definition video and audio streaming, making it ideal for presentations, classrooms, meetings, and home entertainment. With features like multiple simultaneous connections, recording capabilities, and low latency performance, AirServer enhances collaboration and productivity.

It integrates seamlessly with macOS and works reliably on all recent Apple computers, including those with M-series chips. Whether you're teaching a class, hosting a webinar, or sharing media with friends, AirServer delivers a smooth and professional mirroring experience.
